Healthy Life After Transplant

Cleanliness & Hygiene

Maintaining proper cleanliness is one of the most important steps in protecting a transplanted organ. Keeping your surroundings, food, and body clean reduces infection risk and helps you stay healthy.

  • Wash hands regularly with soap.
  • Keep your home clean and dust-free.
  • Avoid contact with people who have infections.
  • Use clean drinking water and freshly cooked food.

Healthy Lifestyle Awareness

After a transplant, a healthy lifestyle is essential for long-term success. Awareness helps you make better choices every day.

  • Follow your doctor's medication schedule strictly.
  • Eat a balanced diet with moderate calories.
  • Get regular medical checkups.
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol.
  • Maintain a healthy weight with light exercise.

Daily Healthy Habits

Simple daily habits can protect your transplant and improve quality of life.

  • Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Take small walks or light exercises regularly.
  • Get 7–8 hours of sleep.
  • Keep stress low with meditation or relaxation.
